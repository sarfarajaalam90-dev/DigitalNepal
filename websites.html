How to Add App to Your Phone

No Play Store needed — install directly from Chrome in seconds

1

Open Chrome on your phone

Make sure you are using Google Chrome browser on your Android or iPhone.

2

Type or paste this link in Chrome: sarfarajaalam90-dev.github.io/DigitalNepal

The Digital Nepal icon will appear on your home screen just like a real app!
